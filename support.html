Frequently Asked Questions
The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?
Please try updating the app to the latest version or restarting your device. If the problem persists, please contact us with your device model.
How do I restore my purchases?
You can usually find a "Restore Purchases" button in the app's Settings menu to retrieve your previous purchases.
How can I request a refund?
For iOS users, please request a refund directly through Apple's "Report a Problem" page. For Android users, please contact Google Play Support.
